## What We're Building

Hasir is an open-source, standalone alternative to [buf.build](https://buf.build) ‚Äî a schema registry designed for teams who want full control over their Protocol Buffer ecosystem.

Built with the powerful [Buf](https://buf.build/docs/ecosystem/cli-overview) toolchain, Hasir provides a self-hosted solution for managing, versioning, and distributing your `.proto` files across your organization.

## Why Hasir?

- **Self-Hosted** ‚Äî Keep your schemas within your infrastructure. No external dependencies, no vendor lock-in.
- **Buf-Powered** ‚Äî Leverages the industry-standard Buf tooling for linting, breaking change detection, and code generation.
- **Schema Versioning** ‚Äî Track changes, manage versions, and ensure backward compatibility across your services.
- **Team Collaboration** ‚Äî Centralized registry for your organization's protobuf definitions.

## Our Vision

We believe schema management should be:

1. **Private by default** ‚Äî Your API contracts are your business
2. **Simple to deploy** ‚Äî Run it anywhere containers run
3. **Developer-friendly** ‚Äî Seamless integration with existing Buf workflows
4. **Open source** ‚Äî Community-driven development
